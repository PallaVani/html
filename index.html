Although all coffee drinks are enjoyed on the basis that they are made from espresso, coffee can be altered to create a world of new flavours and infusions. 1. BLACK COFFEE:

Black coffee, also known as plain straight coffee is brewed simply by pouring hot water over ground coffee beans and allowing it to steep or drip. It can also be prepared with instant coffee powder and hot water.It's served without any additives like milk or sugar, allowing the drinker to experience the pure flavour of the coffee beans. This classic beverage is favoured by those who enjoy the robust and unadulterated taste of coffee.

2. DECAF:

Decaf coffee is made from coffee beans that have undergone a process to remove most of the caffeine content. It is best for people who don’t want caffeine but appreciate the taste of coffee and its flavours.Despite the reduced caffeine, decaf coffee maintains the rich flavour profile of regular coffee, making it a popular choice for those who are sensitive to caffeine or prefer to limit their intake.

3. ESPRESSO:

Espresso is a strong and concentrated coffee beverage made by forcing hot water through finely ground coffee beans at high pressure. Its main feature is its bold flavour and rich crema, the layer of foam that forms on top of the espresso.You can enjoy this beverage both with and without sugar, depending on your preference.Espresso serves as the base for various coffee drinks and is often enjoyed as a quick and intense caffeine boost.

4. LATTE/ICED LATTE:

A latte is a type of coffee beverage made by mixing a strong espresso with milk topped with a thin layer of milk foam. It's a creamy and indulgent coffee beverage with a balanced flavour profile, perfect for those who prefer a smoother coffee experience.This beverage is also extremely versatile when it comes to customization of flavours. An iced latte is simply a latte served over ice, offering a refreshing twist on this classic drink.

5. CAPPUCCINO:

Cappuccino is a traditional beverage originating from Italy, consisting of equal parts espresso, steamed milk, and milk foam. It's well known for its smooth velvety texture and luxurious mouthfeel, achieved by carefully layering the espresso, milk, and foam. Cappuccinos are often adorned with a sprinkle of cocoa powder or cinnamon for added flavour and visual appeal. These flavour additions also make cappuccino extremely versatile when it comes to flavour profiles.

6. MACCHIATO:

A macchiato is an espresso "stained" with a small amount of steamed milk or foam.It strikes a perfect balance between the boldness of espresso and the creaminess of milk, offering a delightful contrast of flavours and textures.Macchiatos can be served hot or iced, making them a great choice for a variety of coffee lovers!

7. AMERICANO:

An Americano is made by diluting a shot of espresso with hot water, resulting in a milder and more approachable coffee beverage. It has a smooth and mild flavour profile, making it a popular choice for those who enjoy the taste of coffee without the intense taste and caffeine boost of espresso.Americanos can be customized with additional water or espresso shots to suit different preferences.

8. CORTADO:

Cortado is a unique coffee beverage made with equal parts espresso and steamed milk. Unlike cappuccinos and lattes, cortados contain minimal foam which allows the bold flavour of espresso to shine through. It's a popular choice for those who enjoy a strong coffee flavour with a smooth and velvety feel.

9. MOCHA LATTE:

Mocha latte combines the rich flavours of espresso, chocolate, and steamed milk and creates a creamy and chocolaty beverage. It's often topped with whipped cream for an extra touch of sweetness and smoothness.Mocha lattes can be customized with different types of chocolate (such as dark, milk, or white) and flavoured syrups for added variety.

10. FLAT WHITE:

Flat white is a smooth and creamy coffee beverage made with espresso and velvety steamed milk. But flat whites have a higher ratio of coffee to milk, resulting in a stronger coffee flavour with a subtle sweetness. It originated in Australia and New Zealand and is characterized by its rich texture and balanced flavour.

11. IRISH COFFEE:

Irish coffee is a delightful blend of hot coffee, Irish whiskey, sugar, and cream, creating a warm, strong and comforting drink to keep you going during the winter season. It's typically served in a heatproof glass or mug and topped with a generous dollop of whipped cream.Irish coffee is perfect for cold evenings or as a festive treat during the holiday season.

12. CAFE AU LAIT:

Cafe au lait, which translates to "coffee with milk" in French, is a simple and elegant coffee beverage made with dark roast coffee and steamed milk. It's a popular choice for breakfast or brunch and pairs well with pastries or croissants. Cafe au lait has a smooth and creamy texture, with a subtle sweetness from the steamed milk.

13. AFFOGATO:

Affogato is a dessert-like coffee made by pouring a shot of hot espresso over a scoop of vanilla ice cream or gelato. The hot espresso melts the ice cream, creating a delightful contrast of hot and cold temperatures. Affogato is a simple yet refreshing treat that's perfect for satisfying sweet cravings or ending a meal on a delicious note.

14. RED EYE:

Red-eye coffee is a bold and sensational beverage made by adding a shot of espresso to a cup of regular coffee. It's popular among coffee enthusiasts who want an extra caffeine kick to start their day. Red-eye coffee is perfect for those mornings when you need an extra boost of energy to get through the day.

15. COLD BREW:

Cold brew is a type of coffee drink that is a trendy and refreshing alternative to hot coffee, made by steeping coarsely ground coffee beans in cold water for long hours.The result is a smooth and flavorful coffee concentrate that can be diluted with water or milk to taste.Cold brew is known for its lower acidity and bitterness compared to traditional hot coffee, making it a popular choice for those with sensitive stomachs or who prefer a smoother coffee.
